Review

From July 4th to July 14th I was on a Royal Caribbean, Liberties of the Seas Cruise. The Cruise left from NJ and went to, Bermuda, Turks & Caicos, and the Dominican Republic. All the islands were very nice with excellent weather and beautiful beaches with white sand, very clear water, and many different plants and flowers. My favorite island was Bermuda with its sandy cliffs and hill sides.
